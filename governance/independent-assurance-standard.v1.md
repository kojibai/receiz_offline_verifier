## Assurance Program

1. External security assessment SHALL run at least annually.
2. Independent control design/operating effectiveness review SHALL run at least annually.
3. Material findings SHALL be entered into corrective-action tracking with target closure dates.
4. Risk acceptance for overdue findings SHALL require Risk Owner sign-off.
5. Annual assurance-cycle status SHALL be recorded in `docs/governance/evidence-register.v1.json`.

## Evidence

Independent assurance artifacts SHALL include scope, period, methodology, findings, and remediation status.
