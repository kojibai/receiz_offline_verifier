# Receiz v55.1.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 16, 2026

This document freezes the primitives and invariants that `v55.1.0` is allowed to extend but not weaken after the `v55.0.0` profile/world/market baseline.

| Primitive | Locked truth | Primary enforcement |
| --- | --- | --- |

## Residual Risks

- Passkey/session propagation is much tighter, but it still depends on browser cookie visibility timing and the bounded client retry window after ceremony completion.
- Magic-link continuation now preserves pending buy context, but it still depends on the mail client reopening the exact continuation URL on the same environment cleanly.
- USD-authoritative settlement is stronger than the earlier path, but it raises the importance of keeping market hydration and wallet history fully aligned with the database settlement source.
- Profile truth reuse is much stronger now, but cross-tab owner/profile mutations can still briefly diverge until the shared workspace snapshot or route snapshot catches up.
- `v55.1.0` is not fully release-cut until public version surfaces, service worker defaults, offline verifier surfaces, and docs all move together to the same version.
