## Manual Launch Tests

### Offline identity

- Create a local Receiz ID from the Receiz Key lane.
- Confirm the signed-in shell/profile seed appears from the local PBI enrollment block without waiting for global publication.
- Confirm failed or delayed publication leaves the local identity intact and pending for later verified append.
- Sign in once with valid local identity proof.
- Disable network.
- Open Account.
- Confirm the local identity remains visible and is not erased by missing session transport.
- Confirm keyfile/PBI-backed continuity remains framed as proof, not as a temporary cache.

### Receipt and verifier proof

- Open a receipt with verified-register-backed proof.
- Confirm proof authority outranks legacy offline evidence.
- Confirm weaker payloads do not replace the visible proof block.
- Confirm offline verifier artifacts show `v66.0.0`.

### Wallet

- Open Wallet after a wallet graph has been stored.
- Confirm Account Value paints from the durable wallet graph immediately.
- Confirm network hydration happens silently.
- Confirm no first-paint balance blank, fake loader, or correction frame appears when local wallet truth exists.

### Market

- Buy and sell from Market.
- Confirm the local position updates immediately.
- Reload Market signed in and confirm active share/prediction positions appear on first paint, before background snapshot enrichment.
- Confirm a lagging snapshot cannot briefly drop the position below the chart or in tracked positions.
- Confirm wallet panel pills remain compact and the three secondary tabs stay in one row on mobile.

### Explore

- Navigate to Explore from a warmed route.
- Confirm useful visible Explore content remains stable while `/api/explore/view` discovers additions.
- Confirm no visible content is replaced by a weaker transport response.

### World and public twin

- Open a public twin that has a local twin mind register.
- Disable network and send a message.
- Confirm the twin replies from local register/model/fallback.
- Re-enable network and send again.
- Confirm the online stream does not silently replace the settled local projection.
- Open a twin without local register truth and confirm the existing online stream path still works.

### Service worker and PWA

- Install/open the PWA online.
- Confirm the current app shell and route snapshots are stored.
- Go offline.
- Confirm deterministic/public snapshots and private account snapshots open from held truth.
- Return online and confirm verified additions sync without visible reload theater.

Confirm public version surfaces, plans/upgrade labels, legal/footer/trust/home/make labels, offline verifier artifacts, service-worker defaults, package metadata, and active release docs all show `v66.0.0`.

## Release Rule

Do not release `v66.0.0` if any test or manual smoke shows:

- local proof waiting on network confirmation before first projection
- anonymous or missing session transport erasing local identity proof
- receipt authority treating weaker offline evidence as equal to verified-register proof
- local twin register projection being silently replaced by online stream output
- wallet first paint dropping a known wallet graph
- market/tracked positions briefly dropping a known local buy or sell
- Explore replacing useful visible content with a weaker payload
- service-worker offline persistence behaving like generic stale cache instead of deterministic/private snapshot truth
- Reserve/Settlement value-loop causality regressing
- version surfaces drifting from `v66.0.0`
