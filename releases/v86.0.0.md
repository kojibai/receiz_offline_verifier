## Title

Receiz `v86.0.0` - Official Locked Proof-Native Baseline

## Summary

`v86.0.0` is the official locked baseline for the full `v80.0.0` to current Receiz arc.

The arc from `v80.0.0` to current `HEAD` spans 551 changed files, 182,894 insertions, and 4,364 deletions before the final release-lock documentation/version edits. The post-`v85.0.0` stabilization arc spans 112 changed files, 9,912 insertions, and 907 deletions before this lock.

The product outcome is now direct:

```txt
Receiz is a proof-native artifact system.
Known proof, ownership, settlement, identity, market, profile, vault, Sports, World, Explore, and public witness truth paints first.
Server, DB, session, global sync, metadata, SEO, analytics, and external systems append or hydrate beneath stronger truth.
```

This release is a baseline, not a feature pile. It locks the present architecture: profile proof objects append to public profile truth and the deterministic market shell; Market keeps instant public truth and signed-in ownership hydration; Explore first-paints from deterministic proof truth; Sports keeps live appends across every relevant game and settles payment-gated Main Event entry correctly; public card QR proof surfaces exist without touching existing vault links; and release law remains executable.

## v80.0.0 Baseline

`v80.0.0` established the game/card/proof foundation that the later releases built on:

- Signal cards were already proof-bearing card objects.
- Card sharing, discovery, wallet activity, profile routes, and public proof surfaces existed.
- The system already carried proof, identity, ownership, and settlement primitives, but several paths still allowed weaker server/session/cache behavior to delay or override stronger truth.

The release work after v80 did not replace that foundation. It made the implemented primitives more explicit, faster, more durable, and harder to regress.

## v81.0.0 - Signal Card Provenance

`v81.0.0` turned Signal cards into stronger provenance objects:

- Signal Card DNA and history became inspectable.
- Rewarded-card payment aligned with pack purchase behavior before reveal.
- Wallet activity and calendar activity reflected card purchases, rewarded cards, and transfers.
- Transfer duplication and repeated receive display issues were repaired.
- Share-card, discovery, card history, mobile chrome, and top-bar popovers were tightened.

The product result: Signal cards carry identity, provenance, ownership activity, and proof history across account, wallet, calendar, and public surfaces.

## v82.0.0 - Sports Arena Becomes A Live Proof Economy

`v82.0.0` launched Sports as a live MLB proof economy:

- Sports cards became ownable proof objects with issue numbers, rarity, player identity, scoring memory, vault state, market value, and verification.
- Packs, free starter cards, reward packs, and odds moved through deterministic issue and claim logic.
- Pick 5, single-game play, Main Event, live cockpit, field view, K-zone, hit trajectory, and scoring context became one Arena.
- Main Event became a daily settlement primitive with entry price, lineup proof, score, rank, payout, refund, and closeout.
- Sports card public pages, vault pages, share actions, listings, trades, event proofs, appends, and transfer routes made the card economy public and inspectable.
- Sports conformance became a release boundary.

The product result: Sports is not a generic game feature. It is a live proof economy where cards, lineups, appends, settlement, vault, and market remain connected.

## v82 Patch Arc - First-Paint And Proof Repairs

The v82 patch arc repaired proof, profile, game, OTC, wallet, and first-paint regressions:

- Live and carried game state stopped replacing current games with weaker placeholders.
- Pregame, final, and selected-game views preserved the correct scoring context.

The rule became operational: the DB is sync and indexing beneath proof truth, not permission for proof truth to display.

`v83.0.0` stabilized the live product:

```txt
paint known truth first
hydrate hidden data next
append verified additions only
never replace a settled shell to prove something already known
```

## Post-v85 Stabilization - The v86 Baseline

The post-v85 arc is the reason this release is the new locked baseline.

### Profile, Market, And Explore

- Public profile posting now appends visible proof objects to canonical public profile truth.
- Visible public profile proof objects are projected into the deterministic Market shell.
- Hidden posts are removed from the local/public market snapshot, and unhidden posts return to Market.
- Cold `/market` first-paints from the current deterministic proof bundle instead of rediscovering profile proof objects before paint.
- Direct Market entry and profile-to-Market navigation converge on the same public proof object set.
- Signed-in Market private graph hydration preserves public truth, owned assets, positions, and history.
- A null or anonymous private snapshot cannot erase signed-in ownership history after the shell paints.
- Explore first-paints from the bundled deterministic market proof object instead of waiting on server discovery.
- Explore preserves profile image data required by username pills without changing Market proof payloads.

### Market Settlement And Chart Surfaces

- Market pill charts and proof-back charts reuse the proven live chart surface instead of maintaining a weaker loading path.
- The chart toolbar is shared and compacted for the popover context.
- The public certificate/share link value path preserves deterministic transport first, then recovers through the existing asset-pill loader when needed.
- Share certificate value uses the same deterministic market math instead of degrading to note-only value.
- Local settlement projection uses the trade quote consistently for wallet/position/history display.

### Sports Arena

- Sports user-live hydration covers every relevant active, zero-append, and recovery source date instead of a capped or forced-today subset.
- Live appends apply to every game that needs them for the day, not only the first small set.
- Pack issuance now includes team-level collector spread so future packs produce better team variety without removing owned-player eligibility.
- Single-card QR public proof route exists for each Sports card.
- Sealed single-card export carries a small QR code to the card-specific public proof route.
- Existing vault card/export/link logic remains untouched.
- Single-card public page can navigate to the owner vault page.
- Main Event checkout cancellation clears pending tournament funding and does not enter the event.
- Successful Main Event payment still verifies paid settlement and enters immediately through the existing continuation.

### Profile Sharing, Player, Calendar, And Copy

- Profile OpenGraph gained a profile-specific proof image path without adding a hot first-paint dependency.
- Player/live sharing keeps the proven playable metadata path and routes click-through to the live player surface.
- Calendar wallet events display correct USD, PHI, and rate truth from the deterministic event/bundle data.
- `/what-can-you-do-with-a-receiz` now opens with the correct thesis: a Receiz is a file that can prove, unlock, identify, settle, and remember.

### World And Public Twin

- World profile and twin surfaces keep stronger preview/portrait truth and avoid stale or weaker presentation taking over first paint.
- Public twin bootstrap and route continuity remain release-contract surfaces.
- Conformance checks remain protected as runtime/source truth, and visual evidence remains release-blocking when it contradicts claimed pass state.

## Product Statement

Receiz is a proof-native artifact system.

Files, identities, ownership states, settlement states, public witness surfaces, verification routes, profiles, Market, Explore, Sports cards, Series Passports, wallet images, Cash Images, OTC upgrades, and card QR proof routes carry product truth.

Server, DB, session, cache, analytics, global sync, SEO, metadata, and external systems may hydrate, append, index, reconcile, and distribute. They do not outrank stronger local, deterministic, sealed, verified, or artifact truth.

`v86.0.0` ships that model as the official locked baseline.
