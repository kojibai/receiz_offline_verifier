## Frozen Invariants

- A settled visible shell is immutable unless the user explicitly interacts.
- Truthful first paint beats richer rediscovery.
- Stronger known deterministic truth is reused before any network rediscovery.
- Weaker partial payloads never downgrade stronger known truth.
- Authenticated/private enrichment may append only; it may not slow or replace the base public shell.
- `/<username>` remains canonical profile truth.
- `/<username>?manage=1` remains canonical profile truth plus owner permissions.
- Business account ownership state must be proven through the account server path, not inferred from richer client UI.
- Ownership transfer proof must preserve the original artifact and receipt proof identity while appending transfer/provenance state.
- Vault imports, decoded records, and offline verifier state must preserve verified record identity across download, verifier, and account surfaces.
- Account brain node selection must append proof-bundle detail without changing the map first paint, and non-openable nodes must remain data-only.
- Account switching must clear or scope profile workspace seeds, wallet route seeds, market route snapshots, world route snapshots, composer projections, vault state, and shared summaries by actor.
- Signed-in market, wallet, account, and profile snapshots must carry enough actor identity to refuse cross-viewer preservation.
- World twin face-feature authority must remain compatible with profile, public twin, route hydration, freshness, generation, and photoreal fit contracts.
- Service-worker and app-version defaults must match the release version.

pnpm test:world-release-contracts

The full release-freeze script also runs product inventory, deterministic surfaces, page-first-paint, artifact binding, document seal, world trail package, golden path, contact contracts, signature, API security, chaos failure, value-loop, economy, market, verification, world, profile, chat, performance, and governance gates.

## Residual Risks

- The release is not fully locked until `pnpm test:release-freeze` passes after the `v73.0.0` surface bump.
- `pnpm release:attest` depends on release attestation signing environment outside the codebase.
- The v72-to-v73 diff includes committed Playwright evidence artifacts under `.playwright-cli/` and `output/playwright/`; those files are evidence outputs, not runtime product surfaces.
- Production database, Redis, Stripe, and external provider health remain deployment concerns, but they must not become permission to believe already-held local proof.
