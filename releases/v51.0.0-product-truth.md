# Receiz v51.0.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 12, 2026

This document freezes the primitives and invariants that `v51.0.0` is allowed to extend but not weaken.

## Frozen Primitives

| Primitive | Locked truth | Primary enforcement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| File-authoritative trust core | The file remains the authority. Deterministic manifests remain the authority for route truth, and ledger-backed settlement remains the authority for payment truth. Pricing, CRM, and world layers operate around those boundaries, not in place of them. | `app/lib/profile/document.ts`, `app/lib/profile/server.ts`, `app/lib/receiptView.ts`, `app/lib/wallet/payIntent.ts`, `app/api/wallet/ledger/public/route.ts` |
| Public ladder matches product truth | The public plan story is `Free -> Authorship -> Business -> Automation -> Enterprise`. That ladder must remain the primary way the product is explained on upgrade and adjacent plan-facing surfaces. | `app/upgrade/page.tsx`, `app/upgrade/layout.tsx`, `app/upgrade/opengraph-image.tsx`, `app/lib/premium/plan.ts` |
| Stable internal billing keys | Internal subscription and entitlement keys remain `free`, `premium`, `business`, and `pro`. Public naming may evolve, but checkout, sync, and entitlement persistence cannot silently break because of front-facing copy changes. | `app/lib/premium/plan.ts`, `app/api/stripe/checkout/route.ts`, `app/lib/premium/stripeSync.ts`, `app/lib/premium/getPlan.ts` |
| Free remains genuinely usable | Free must remain a complete public surface with public presence, scheduling, messaging, payments, and verification. Paid tiers may open deeper operating layers, but they must not reduce Free into a dead-end teaser. | `app/upgrade/page.tsx`, `app/components/layout/GlobalAuthChrome.tsx`, `app/lib/world/publicTwinNarrative.ts` |
| Authorship / Business / Automation stay distinct | Authorship is proof and custody. Business is ownership of the branded surface. Automation is the active operating layer with memory, timing, sequencing, and action. These tiers must not collapse back into a generic feature pile. | `app/lib/premium/plan.ts`, `app/upgrade/page.tsx`, `app/account/page.tsx`, `app/what-is-a-receiz/page.tsx` |
| WireProof is included, not the top-level story | WireProof remains included where appropriate, but it must not become the headline frame for the Automation tier. | `app/upgrade/page.tsx`, `app/wireproof/page.tsx`, `app/faq/page.tsx` |
| Full server-truth Contacts visibility | Signed-in Contacts must load the real owner CRM, not an arbitrary first chunk or stale browser-only snapshot. | `app/api/contacts/route.ts`, `app/contacts/page.tsx`, `app/contacts/ContactsPageExperience.tsx`, `app/lib/contacts/server.ts` |
| Clean CRM identity floor | A contact must not remain an active CRM row if it has no meaningful name/company, no reachable identity, no linked user, and no continuity anchor. Placeholder `Receiz contact` junk cannot be treated as real operator records. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts` |
| Progressive person continuity | Guest, authenticated, booking, and campaign identities must be able to converge into one contact record over time instead of fragmenting across tables and sessions. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts`, `app/lib/world/guestProfile.ts`, `app/lib/world/runtime.ts`, `supabase/migrations/20260412151000_world_guest_profiles.sql` |
| World stays a live operator surface | `/world` must remain a live operator surface with truthful campaign state, next-best moves, reply work, and activity continuity. | `app/world/WorldExperience.tsx`, `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/lib/world/types.ts` |
| User-facing history and activity copy | Contacts history, Calendar notes, and world activity feeds must stay front-facing and useful. Provider names, route paths, deterministic keys, hashes, and raw schema/event plumbing may be stored, but they must not be the primary visible operator copy. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts`, `app/lib/calendar/server.ts`, `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/world/WorldExperience.tsx` |
| Release-governed version surfaces | Package version, service worker version, public badges, offline verifier surfaces, and release docs must move together. | `scripts/check_release_lock.mjs` |

## Frozen Invariants

| Invariant | What must not regress | Locked by |
| --- | --- | --- |
| No hidden CRM cap on the owner list | `/contacts` must continue loading beyond a single backend page and must not silently stop at a round-number ceiling. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts`, `app/contacts/__tests__/contactsBootstrapContract.test.ts` |
| Placeholder contacts do not pollute active CRM | Calendar or system sync cannot keep minting active contacts that only say `Receiz contact` and contain no meaningful identity. Existing invalid rows must stay archived out of the active CRM. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts`, `app/contacts/__tests__/contactsBootstrapContract.test.ts` |
| World leads stay visible through Contacts | If a campaign contact exists in the owner CRM, the owner-facing Contacts experience must remain able to surface it directly. | `app/contacts/page.tsx`, `app/api/contacts/[contactId]/route.ts`, `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/contacts/__tests__/contactsBootstrapContract.test.ts` |
| Signed-in pricing surfaces do not leak legacy naming | Upgrade, account, and tier-facing surfaces must not keep exposing `Premium` and `Pro` as the main public plan names. | `app/upgrade/page.tsx`, `app/account/page.tsx`, `app/wireproof/page.tsx`, `app/faq/page.tsx`, `app/what-is-a-receiz/page.tsx` |
| Signed-out users can understand the ladder | A first-time visitor on the pricing surface must be able to understand the plan architecture before authentication. | `app/upgrade/page.tsx` |
| Activity timelines stay user-facing | Activity and history surfaces must keep showing human-readable status plus actual sent copy where available, without leaking provider plumbing as operator-facing text. | `app/lib/contacts/server.ts`, `app/lib/calendar/server.ts`, `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/lib/calendar/__tests__/eventSanitization.test.ts`, `app/lib/world/__tests__/campaignRevenueEngineContract.test.ts` |
| World counts do not promise unsendable work | Deal-room and campaign counts may be live, but they must not oscillate meaninglessly or represent blocked work as ready-now work. | `app/world/WorldExperience.tsx`, `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/lib/world/__tests__/campaignRevenueEngineContract.test.ts` |

| Same-day repeat outreach stays cooled down | The system must continue preferring fresh reachable leads over same-day repeat touches to the same person. | `app/lib/world/campaigns.ts`, `app/lib/world/outreachDelivery.ts`, `app/lib/world/__tests__/campaignRevenueEngineContract.test.ts` |
| Release surface inventory stays canonical | The committed release note, checklist, product-truth doc, and public version surfaces must align with package and service worker truth. | `scripts/check_release_lock.mjs` |

## Release Gates

`v51.0.0` is considered locked only if these pass together:

- `pnpm exec tsc --noEmit --pretty false`
- `npx tsx --test app/lib/premium/__tests__/plan.test.ts`
- `pnpm test:contacts-release-contracts`
- `pnpm test:world-release-contracts`
- `npx tsx --test app/contacts/__tests__/contactsBootstrapContract.test.ts app/lib/world/__tests__/campaignRevenueEngineContract.test.ts`
- `pnpm release:lock`
- `pnpm test:release-freeze`
- `pnpm release:attest`
- `pnpm release:verify-attestation <path>`

The manual release procedure is defined in `docs/releases/v51.0.0-checklist.md`.

## Residual Risks

- The public ladder is now consistent in the main upgrade flow and key adjacent surfaces, but older long-tail docs can still drift if they are updated outside the shared plan mapping helpers.
- Internal billing keys remain stable by design, which means Stripe price configuration must be checked carefully against the public ladder before deployment.
- Guest profile continuity is materially stronger, but production quality still depends on first-party identity capture actually being present in live traffic.
- Contacts placeholder cleanup now archives obvious junk rows out of the active CRM, but legacy rows with partial identity may still require operator review rather than blind automated deletion.
- Provider and outreach history is now much more user-facing, but old records cannot show message copy that was never persisted.
