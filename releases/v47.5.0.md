# Receiz v47.5.0

Release date: April 9, 2026

## Overview

`v47.5.0` is the world revenue-operations release. It turns the public twin from a conversational surface into a real operator surface by adding revenue skills, multi-channel outbound opportunity lanes, owner-side revenue playbooks, proof-first record kits, unified action-card embeds, booking confirmation packs, and post-call closeout rails without weakening the existing trust or payment primitives.

The product-truth freeze for this release is defined in `docs/releases/v47.5.0-product-truth.md`.

## What Defines v47.5.0

- World twins can now run explicit revenue skills on top of the existing action rails: qualify, match, quote, deposit, proof, handoff, follow-up, and renewal.
- `/world` now gives owners a revenue-operations control surface instead of only a policy toggle list.
- Outbound communication is now first-class across Receiz email, SMS, and app-webhook channels, all tied to saved owner policy.
- Operator playbooks turn post-call closeout, warm lead follow-up, and renewal nudges into one-step draft loads instead of manual composition.
- Purpose-specific proof kits, action-card embeds, booking confirmation packs, call closeout packs, and the developer site kit make the surrounding business surface more productized and easier to deploy.
- Release-governed version surfaces stay locked together across package metadata, service worker defaults, public badges, offline verifier surfaces, and release documents.

## Targeted Release Outcomes

### 1. World Can Now Sell, Close, And Route Safely

The public twin no longer stops at generic scheduling and payment talk:

- revenue skills are stored as explicit owner policy, normalized server-side, and grounded into runtime prompt context
- deterministic world reply fallbacks now understand quote, deposit, proof, follow-up, renewal, and team-routing lanes
- public twin action rails can now surface `quote`, `deposit`, `proof`, and `renew` alongside the older intake / routing / handoff controls

### 2. Owner-Side Revenue Operations Are Faster

Owners now get a real revenue-operating console inside `/world`:

- the agent tab exposes a compact revenue-skills section with a recommended conversion stack
- operator playbooks load post-call, warm-follow-up, and renewal drafts directly into the outreach composer
- the playbooks carry the best available next-step route using existing payment, scheduling, proof, and public-twin paths

### 3. Outbound Communication Is Now A Real Twin Surface

Outbound moved from an idea into a governed product surface:

- Receiz email sends a real outbound email and opens a secure Receiz reply thread
- SMS sends through the configured relay and routes the recipient back into the twin
- app-webhook delivery pushes structured outreach into Slack, Discord, CRM, Zapier, Make, or similar systems
- all three channels remain explicitly tied to the saved world policy rather than unsaved local state

### 4. The Surrounding Business Surface Is More Productized

The release also tightens the business-adjacent surfaces around the twin:

- proof-kit landing pages now open preloaded record flows instead of blank generic forms
- the unified action-card embed brings ask / book / pay into one deployable surface
- booking confirmation packs and call closeout packs make follow-through more operational instead of conversational
- the developer site kit packages login, verify, pay, and schedule primitives into a clearer adoption surface

## Deployment Env For This Release

The release introduces live twin outreach channels. Production configuration now needs:

- Receiz email: `RESEND_API_KEY`, `RECEIZ_FROM_EMAIL`
- SMS relay: `RECEIZ_WORLD_TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID` or `TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID`; `RECEIZ_WORLD_TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN` or `TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN`; and either `RECEIZ_WORLD_TWILIO_FROM_PHONE` / `TWILIO_FROM_PHONE` or `RECEIZ_WORLD_TWILIO_MESSAGING_SERVICE_SID` / `TWILIO_MESSAGING_SERVICE_SID`
- Favorite-app webhooks: no required deployment env, but production destinations should be controlled, signed, and owned by the operator
