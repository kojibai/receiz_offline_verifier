# Receiz v52.5.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 13, 2026
Status: Locked for `v52.5.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v52.5.0`. It carries forward the existing file-authoritative, route-deterministic, settlement-truth, and governed-market model from `v52.0.0` while recording deterministic quote math, replayable quote evidence, bounded spread/pressure behavior, partial share-certificate custody, first-class market conformance, lighter premium market runtime, contextual market media behavior, inline launch flows, and official market share surfaces as explicit non-regression behavior at release time.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, and visible version-surface alignment only. The verifier trust contract remains unchanged relative to `v52.0.0`.

## Core Authority Boundaries

The release does not relax the core Receiz authority model:

- The file remains the authority for artifact truth.
- Deterministic manifests remain the authority for historical route truth.
- Ledger-backed settlement remains the authority for payment truth.
- Market remains the governed execution layer around those truths rather than a replacement for them.
- Quote math, media handling, share surfaces, and inline launch flows must continue to respect those authorities rather than bypass them.
- Offline verifier entrypoints, release metadata, and public release documents remain transport and governance around those boundaries, not replacements for them.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. The Deterministic Quote Engine Must Remain Canonical

- A canonical quote model version must continue to define market execution behavior.
- Asset execution must continue to resolve from deterministic inputs into reference mid, quote mid, bid, ask, spread basis points, and shift basis points.
- Buy/sell previews must continue to align with the same quote surface used by execution when quote data is available.

### 2. Spread, Pressure, And Quote Evidence Must Stay Bounded And Replayable

- Market sustainability must continue to be expressed through quote-embedded spread behavior rather than a separately layered visible runtime fee.
- Inventory imbalance, recent flow, and volatility pressure must remain explicit bounded inputs into quote shaping.
- Spread and shift behavior must continue to obey hard-cap and execution-guard rules rather than drift into ad hoc economics.
- Executed trades must continue to persist replayable quote evidence including model version, price fields, spread/shift fields, and pressure inputs.

### 3. Share Certificates Must Support Partial Custody Motion

- Share certificates must continue to support partial withdrawals rather than all-or-nothing movement.
- Certificates must continue to track remaining balances on the certificate itself.
- Redeposit flows must continue to support all-or-part returns into platform custody.
- Share quantity and market value continuity must remain coherent across custody export and import paths.

### 4. Market Conformance Must Remain First-Class

- Market must continue to ship with its own conformance suite, badge, history, and rollup endpoints.
- Market conformance must continue to validate deterministic quote behavior, bounded spread/shift rules, execution-preview alignment, persisted quote evidence, and required source-trace inclusion.
- Release attestation and broader trust surfaces must continue to treat market as its own governed suite rather than hiding it behind settlement.

### 5. Premium Market Surfaces Must Stay Lighter And More Deliberate

- Pulse-only market surfaces must continue to avoid countdown-driven rerender paths when they do not render countdowns.
- Market pills must continue to use an asset-scoped lightweight lane before waking the full desk snapshot.
- Market bootstrap work must remain aggressively parallelized.
- `/market` formatting must remain deterministic so the route does not regress into locale/timezone hydration mismatch.
- Value-pill surfaces must remain smaller, sigil-driven, draggable, and free of backdrop blur while preserving direct market actions.
- Market audio chrome must continue to inherit the active asset palette instead of falling back to the generic global skin.

### 6. Launch And Media Must Stay Inside Governed Market Flows

- The launch panel must continue to create sealed records/assets and ventures inline from the market desk rather than forcing cross-surface handoffs.
- Media actions from market assets must continue to hand off into the platform's existing audio/video, image, document, and ebook primitives rather than inventing a separate behavior stack.
- Market audio playback must continue to hand the global player a real market queue so continuity can outlive the current asset.

### 7. Official Market Share Surfaces Must Remain Dedicated And Truthful

- Shared market assets must continue to resolve through `/market/assets/[receizId]` rather than a generic desk URL.
- The share route must continue to carry asset-specific metadata instead of inheriting the generic market page treatment.
- Market share cards for Open Graph and Twitter must continue to read as an official market surface rather than a recycled artifact card.

### 8. Release Governance And Version Truth

- Release-scoped note and product-truth documents are required for `v52.5.0`.
- Release-governed version surfaces must move together across current-release docs and shipped offline HTML entrypoints.
- This repository records the public release documents and shipped HTML version markers for that lockstep requirement.

## Allowed Evolution

Implementation details may change after `v52.5.0` only if they preserve the authority boundaries and locked release behaviors above. If a future change materially alters one of these truths, it should ship under a new explicit release note and updated product-truth freeze.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v52.5.0` if it:

- removes the canonical quote model as the authority for market execution behavior
- reintroduces a separate visible runtime fee story on top of execution instead of quote-embedded spread capture
- makes spread/shift pressure opaque, unbounded, or no longer explainable from persisted evidence
- breaks partial share-certificate withdrawal, remaining-balance tracking, or partial redeposit continuity
- downgrades market conformance, release attestation, or trust-row treatment back into a settlement-only story
- regresses market pills, floating-card behavior, runtime quietness, or hydration-safe formatting into more wasteful or less coherent behavior
- breaks inline launch continuity, contextual media handoff, or market-queue playback continuity
- removes the dedicated official market share route or its asset-specific metadata/card treatment
- changes release truth without updating the versioned release docs and aligned version surfaces
