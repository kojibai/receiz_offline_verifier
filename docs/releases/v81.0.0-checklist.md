- Known verified truth moves first, later network work appends verified additions, and visible surfaces do not rediscover or downgrade settled truth.
- Signal Cards remain owned Receiz assets whose DNA/history context is additive around a stable sealed claim.
- `/game/cards/history` exposes origin, owner context, listings, auctions, bids, sales, verified arena appends, metrics, and price points without mutating card truth.
- Card surfaces expose explicit paths to share pages, card-world swipe view, DNA/history, and Find Cards without making users guess where provenance lives.

- Card receive events deduplicate so one movement appears once.
- Card history reads across all discoverable cards, while Card World remembers only explicit same-session expansion.
- Explorer, discovery, card backs, proof manifests, popovers, and Find Cards entry paths remain readable and fit the available surface.
- Mobile game chrome, dark/white route coloring, nav hit targets, card-share animation, liquid loaders, and rewarded-card stage sizing remain stable.
- Signal Card art, orb realism, outer rim treatment, and proof-manifest layout improve asset quality without hiding readable proof facts.
- Release surfaces move together to `v81.0.0`.
