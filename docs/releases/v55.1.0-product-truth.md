# Receiz v55.1.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 16, 2026
Status: Locked for `v55.1.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v55.1.0`. It carries forward the existing file-authoritative, deterministic-route, and wallet-and-ledger-backed model from `v55.0.0` while recording reusable profile truth, deterministic route handoff, passkey-first market continuation, exact-USD non-entitled settlement, and tighter live-player presentation as explicit non-regression behavior at release time.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, and visible version-surface alignment only. The verifier trust contract remains unchanged relative to `v55.0.0`.

## Core Authority Boundaries

The release does not relax the core Receiz authority model:

- File-backed proof remains the authority for artifact truth.
- Deterministic route state and already-known route snapshots remain the authority for first paint and navigation continuity truth.
- Wallet, ledger, and exact USD execution state remain the authority for money truth.
- Live session, market, player, media, and world deltas may hydrate on top of deterministic baseline state, but they must not replace or discard that baseline as the source of truth.
- Public product routes, admin surfaces, release metadata, analytics, and offline verifier entrypoints remain operating or governance surfaces around those authorities, not replacements for them.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. Profile Truth Must Stay A Reusable Live Graph

- `/profile`, the owner workspace, public profiles, search handoff, the global composer host, and profile navigation must continue to reuse already-known owner or route truth instead of re-resolving through extra shells.
- Reactive session-bootstrap hydration must continue to update signed-in chrome immediately after passkey-created sessions.
- Public profile hydration must continue to keep client-only seeds out of the initial render tree while still landing with complete header media and joined metadata.
- Local profile publish continuity must continue to update the active profile graph instead of immediately replacing it with a weaker refetch path.

### 2. Known Route Truth Must Continue Across Navigation

- Profile artifact/live-player actions, floating people search, bottom-bar market/profile/world routes, account-to-dashboard entry, market fast-entry handoff, and chart-to-market `Full` transitions must continue to warm and reuse the richest known route snapshot.
- Route feedback must remain on-button and tied to real pending state rather than dead taps or artificial waits.
- The product must not regress into second-click or second-settle behavior where known truth is discarded and rediscovered after navigation.

### 3. Market Buy Must Stay One Continuation Ceremony

- Buy from chart and full desk must continue to start as one passkey-first action.
- Passkey success must continue to resolve in place, and retry-based session pickup must continue to prefer the newly created session before falling back to email.
- Funding must continue to cover only the exact shortfall, use the dedicated checkout continuation, and resume the same pending order automatically after settlement.
- Closing funding after passkey must not sign the user back out or lose the pending trade context.

### 4. Sell And Execution Must Stay USD-Intent Authoritative

- Sell must continue to be budget-first on chart and desk, using USD receive input with share quantity derived from the live bid and clamped to held position.
- The sell lane must exist only when the viewer actually holds shares in that asset.
- Share buy and prediction wager execution must continue to preserve locked USD spend intent and derive resulting share or position outcomes from that budget.
- Non-entitled wallet execution, availability, next-wallet value, and settlement must continue to derive from exact USD balances and entitlement metadata rather than loose phi inference.
- Share-certificate trading, import, and market-position hydration must remain aligned with the USD-authoritative server path.

### 5. Live Player And Chart Presentation Must Stay Deterministic

- Live-player entry must continue to preserve continuity through prepared route handoff.
- The back card must fit inside frame without clipped copy or required internal scroll.
- Public twin preview precedence must continue to prefer a valid public-ready twin over stale hidden preview state.
- Chart pill height and visible lanes must continue to reflect real available actions, with Sell absent for viewers who do not hold the asset.

### 6. Release Governance And Version Truth

- Release-scoped note, product-truth, and checklist documents are required for `v55.1.0`.
- Release-governed version surfaces must move together across current-release docs, verifier-contract docs, and shipped offline HTML entrypoints.
- This repository records the public release documents and shipped HTML version markers for that lockstep requirement.

## Allowed Evolution

Implementation details may change after `v55.1.0` only if they preserve the authority boundaries and locked release behaviors above. If a future change materially alters one of these truths, it should ship under a new explicit release note and updated product-truth freeze.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v55.1.0` if it:

- reintroduces profile-shell drift or makes profile, public-profile, search, or composer flows rediscover already-known owner truth through weaker shells
- discards known route snapshots and forces a second click, second settle, or route-loading shell for actions that already have deterministic truth
- splits buy back into separate auth, funding, and trade detours or loses continuation after passkey or checkout
- reverts sell or non-entitled execution away from exact USD intent, exact USD settlement, or held-position gating
- shows Sell for viewers who do not actually hold shares in the asset
- regresses live-player back-card fit, public twin preview precedence, or prepared player route handoff continuity
- changes release truth without updating the versioned release docs and aligned version surfaces
