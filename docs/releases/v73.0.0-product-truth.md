# Receiz v73.0.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 29, 2026
Status: Locked for `v73.0.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v73.0.0`, the ownership, vault, and continuity release after the `v72.0.0` stable continuity baseline.

`v73.0.0` extends product coverage for business account ownership, receipt provenance, vault portability, account brain proof-bundle detail, authority navigation, and post-v72 continuity contracts. It does not weaken the `v70.0.0` public alpha law.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, shipped HTML version markers, and package-metadata alignment only. The verifier proof-format and producer-payload contract remain unchanged relative to `v60.0.0`.

## Core Product Truth

`v73.0.0` carries forward the `v70.0.0` public alpha law and the `v72.0.0` stable continuity locks:

> Identity is local. Proof is in the file. Verified truth is append-only. `/<username>` is canonical profile truth. Settlement and Reserve preserve funded causality. The network propagates and appends; it does not get to downgrade stronger proof already held by the device.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. Canonical Profile And Owner Continuity Remain Law

- `/<username>` remains canonical profile truth.
- `/<username>?manage=1` remains owner permissions only.
- Owner/private enrichment must not make signed-in profile first paint slower than public profile truth.
- Account page and one-viewport continuity fixes remain product law, not cosmetic layout preference.

### 2. Business Account Ownership Is Account Truth

- Business account entity ownership is first-class account truth.
- Server APIs must expose business ownership behavior explicitly.
- Certificate generation and holdings reads must be covered as business ownership contracts.
- Business ownership additions must not create a competing identity or artifact truth.

### 3. Receipt Ownership Provenance Is Verified Addition

- Receipt ownership provenance and transfer proof are verified additions.
- Transfer flows must carry stronger proof bundles and route-level continuity.
- Provenance and transfer proof must not rewrite artifact identity.
- Provenance and transfer proof must not rewrite canonical receipt truth.

### 4. Vault And Original Downloads Preserve Portable Records

- Vault decode, verifier, and download state must preserve portable verified record truth.
- Recovered artifact state must not degrade into disposable UI state.
- Original-download paths must preserve verified record portability.
- Download and vault continuity must remain route-addressable when route truth exists.

### 5. Account Brain Proof Detail Is Selected-Node Data

- Account brain selections expose compact proof-bundle data.
- The proof detail popover must be dismissible and mobile-friendly.
- Proof-bundle detail is selected-node data, not first-paint loading theater.
- Every selected node shows proof data.
- Open is shown only for nodes that have a real route target.

### 6. Authority Navigation Names The Offline Surface

- Account authority navigation names the offline surface as Offline Verifier.
- Privacy and Terms consolidate into Legal.
- Security remains one tap away.
- Navigation labels must describe authority surfaces plainly.

### 7. World Twin Fit Remains Bounded

- World twin face feature authority remains bounded by explicit contracts.
- Photoreal shell fit remains bounded by contracts, not broad viewport guesses.
- Post-v72 account, profile, composer, chat, discovery, and world twin UI improvements remain under explicit continuity and fit contracts.

### 8. Version Surfaces Move Together

- Package metadata, service-worker defaults, public version badges, verifier surfaces, README release pointer, release notes, changelog, docs indexes, and release docs must agree on `v73.0.0`.
- Historical release documents preserve their own version truth.

## Important Non-Changes

- The verifier proof-format and producer-payload contract remain unchanged.
- Trusted-signature success still requires verified `signatureV4`.
- Effective anchor context remains required for verified outcomes.
- Groth16 fields remain required and only real `g16:` proof payloads are accepted.
- The `v64.0.0` value-loop invariants remain in force.
- The `v70.0.0` public alpha invariant register remains the baseline law.
- The `v72.0.0` stable continuity locks remain in force.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v73.0.0` if it:

- weakens `/<username>` canonical profile truth or turns owner mode into a competing profile
- treats business ownership as decorative UI instead of account truth
- rewrites artifact identity or canonical receipt truth through provenance or transfer proof
- degrades vault or original-download verified records into transient transport state
- shows account brain Open actions for nodes without real route targets
- hides selected-node proof data behind first-paint loading theater
- renames or buries the Offline Verifier authority surface
- replaces bounded world twin face and photoreal fit contracts with broad viewport guesses
- allows version drift between package metadata, README, public badges, verifier surfaces, and release docs
