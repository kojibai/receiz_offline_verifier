## Canonical Product Gates

- Signal Circuit opens as a dedicated `/game` surface with no fake loader shell.
- Signal Card issue paths use existing Receiz proof primitives and preserve separated achievement truth and transferable collectible truth.
- Signal Card artwork is generated, stored, replayed, exported, and protected from stale cached downgrade.
- Downloaded verified card fronts use `☤KAI` visible copy instead of old Pulse framing.
- Signal Pack purchase and reveal flows are wallet-aware, exact-rate, resumable, settlement-safe, and incomplete until sealed cards are issued, stored, and recoverable.
- Signal Vault sync is account-scoped, paginated, retryable, batch-hydrated, and protected against account bleed.
- Card transfer/listing/auction/purchase/battle surfaces append market, audit, battle, and progression rows without mutating original card claims.
- Card public/share/OpenGraph/Twitter/proof/reserve-first purchase surfaces render from sealed card truth without liquid loader or placeholder replacement paths.
- Signal Arena battle history remains append-only and AI twin defense requires owner opt-in.
- Signal Pack Foundry provides tiered brand pack campaigns with visible odds, supply caps, pack limits, arena/AR options, manifest seals, campaign records, and verification isolation.
- Signal Circuit conformance publishes requirements, claim-to-code checks, source digests, scarcity proof, bounded simulation, badges, history, rollups, and status inventory together.
- Public/developer Status includes Signal Circuit and high-value public card/conformance endpoints while excluding private vault, pack purchase, admin, webhook, worker, and legacy keyring surfaces.
- Creator Workspace is first-class `/creator`, first-paints settled local workspace truth, uses Streamdown for creator output, and keeps private actions behind explicit paths.
- Creator OS durable queues and records exist for project, brand, run, message, event, output, asset, version, usage-ledger, publish-job, and safety-review storage.
- Creator paid production debits wallet value idempotently before background production continues, and shortfalls open embedded wallet funding.
- Creator managed launch records GitHub/Vercel/Vercel Sandbox provider proof and durable launch output without route escape.
- Verified Data Vault links bytes before sealing, exports verifiable ZIP/vault packages, restores original files, and previews restored bytes.
- Old Safari document sealing uses Signature V4 online and fails closed offline with Safari 17+ or online guidance, with no legacy V3 fallback.
- Runtime stale recovery clears stale runtime state silently and returns to stable paths without automatic root interstitials or stale-guard redirects.
- Market share quote bootstrap batches long asset lists before Supabase `in` filters.
- Wallet/account/profile/original continuity preserves canonical truth and local proof authority.
- Release-surface lockstep includes package metadata, readmes, changelog/release notes, and shipped HTML version markers.
