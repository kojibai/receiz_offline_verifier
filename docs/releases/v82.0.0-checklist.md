## Canonical Product Gates

- Sports is a first-class Receiz destination with `/game/sports`, `/game/sports/baseball`, live MLB rows, Pick 5, live field, cockpit, leaders, proofs, cards, profile access, and mobile bottom chrome.
- MLB cards are owned, issued, playable inventory with deterministic issue numbers, pack origin, owner, history, live career, and market path.
- Player strength and card rarity remain separate gates; base-set catalog cards do not show fake rarity labels.
- Published pack odds, observed simulation output, player scarcity, rarity availability, duplicate pressure, top-player concentration, spread, and registry basis hashes remain inspectable.
- Per-game play and Main Event have separate lock domains, selection scopes, button language, cockpit context, finality, and reward claims.
- Pick 5 selected/unselected card ordering and score context stay clear for selected game, date, and mode.
- Live cockpit shows selected scoring cards, scoped deltas, score total, rank, payout proximity, scoring story, movement charting, and value cues.
- Selected game field data must match the selected game. Entry lock state, active entries, and finalized proof fallback must not rewrite live scoreboards.
- Pregame field state shows matchup readiness without pretending live play happened.
- Daily tournament entry, wallet funding, Stripe delta recovery, minimum participants, underfill refunds, top-percent payout, result sharing, settlement payout, and play-again reset work as one economy.
- Finality can rank, issue rewards, open reward packs, update proof history, append card-career memory, and clear claimed proof surfaces.
- Sports card career appends preserve arena entry, rank, reward, score, source date, game key, lineup scope, append hash, provenance, value, and ownership context.
- Sports market supports sales, wallet-first purchase, Stripe delta funding, provenance, value panels, bids, asks, floors, spread, volume, movement, listings, sale history, and market conformance.
- Sports trading guards accept, decline, cancel, and counterproposal flows, including `409 sports_trade_not_open` for non-open decline/cancel and `500 sports_trade_lookup_failed` for unexpected lookup failures.
- Sports conformance publishes game-child routes, badge, history, rollups, rarity scoring, odds, source checks, hot fanout checks, checkout delta recovery checks, and signed history checks.
- Scale hardening uses public hot snapshots, warmed state, runtime cache, stream fanout, account overlay throttling, route backpressure, worker jobs, observability, and build-cost controls.
- PWA and first-paint state project account, joined, cockpit, card catalog, signing, and optimistic post-anchor truth without refresh rituals.
- Release surfaces move together to `v82.0.0`.
