## Locked Product Truth

- Receiz remains a proof-native artifact system, not a generic app with proof features.
- `/api/receiz/anchor` is the foreground proof publication route and owns the readable anchor response contract.
- The local signed-action outbox is a weaker replay/sync/publication layer and may not admit, sign, wrap, consume, inspect, or replay the foreground anchor response path before the composer reads it.
- Profile original publication is complete only when it appends or refreshes canonical profile showcase truth, public profile market append truth, and visible local route snapshots.
- The sealed Sports Vault image/manifest is the proof object. Server publish, showcase, and global sync may append beneath it, but may not replace it.
- Sports cards, packs, lineups, vault objects, event proofs, FIFA/UFC/fight additions, and public showcases are proof objects.
- Receiz ID, PBI, wallet settlement, wallet ledger receipts, and durable runtime projection remain identity and settlement primitives.
- Service-worker/cache mechanics may warm and activate, but they do not redefine signed-in proof continuity, settled first paint, or release truth.
- Lease cockpit, market ownership, sale, claim, and transaction room paths remain ownership and settlement primitives.
- Faster DB/server reads are valid only when they preserve stronger proof/local truth and append verified additions.
- Regression lessons and commit-history boundaries are release truth and must stay required by `release:lock`.

## Forbidden Downgrades

A change is out of bounds for `v89.0.0` if it:

- re-admits `/api/receiz/anchor` to a broad `/api/receiz` outbox classifier.
- downgrades foreground anchor publication to generic local signed-action outbox behavior.
- downgrades the anchor response contract to an unreadable replay envelope.
- treats a profile original DB row as globally complete without showcase, market append, and visible route snapshot projection.
- downgrades a Sports Vault sealed image or manifest to a browser upload payload.
- downgrades Sports proof objects, FIFA/UFC/fight event proofs, vault objects, cards, packs, or lineups to generic cards.
- downgrades wallet settlement, Receiz ID, or PBI truth to account display state.
- downgrades lease, market ownership, sale, claim, or transaction room paths to generic listings.
- downgrades deterministic first paint to loading state.
- downgrades verified history to activity feed.
- downgrades service-worker release truth to incidental cache behavior.
- lets external systems define Receiz truth instead of providing implementation mechanics.
- ships version drift between package metadata, release docs, README/release notes, static entrypoints, service-worker runtime, badges, or release markers.
