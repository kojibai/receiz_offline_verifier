## Frozen Invariants

### Identity and profile behavior

The identity primitive is the accepted proof and admitted same-account continuity, including PBI, Receiz ID, Receiz Key, Identity Record, Identity Seal, recovered-account continuity, and an established admitted session after proof acceptance.

Inline username changes and full profile saves use `PATCH /api/profile/me`. The already-admitted account is the actor and its UID never changes during rename. Commit-time coordination decides global username uniqueness; only after commitment is the prior username released and the same account's session, profile, and public proof projection refreshed.

An absent, delayed, or failed `identity_keys` projection cannot deny a first-party save, select another identity, or be translated into `identity_key_mismatch`. The first-party operation does not ask `/api/receiz/identity/profile`, the SDK, MCP, or AI which identity is active. This is the exact behavior at `a1081796e90a65124d952ce5907512999dd16cb0`, before `9611cb8f57c587eae1b6ee8079ee6d7bd2a02637` introduced the regression.
