## What Regressed

v110 allowed structural admission fabrication and terminal MCP confirmation reuse, and did not require independent evidence roots for caller-supplied recovery history.

## Why It Regressed

Runtime-issued plans were protected while the admission phase and history roots were not bound completely back to exact verified artifact truth.

- Runtime custody is anti-forgery mechanics, never the primitive or the reason authority is valid.
- Comparing a stored admission digest is circular; planning must canonically reverify retained exact bytes and recompute the basis.
- A normalized proof graph is inspection material until every authority-bearing node has an independently verified stronger evidence root.
- Parseable key material does not grant `canSign`; private-key control must be proven against the bound public identity.
- Deterministic operation identity and a consumable execution attempt are different concepts.
- Constitutional laws must scope their denial predicates to the governed operation; an artifact law must not deny unrelated lawful commands.
- Compatibility can preserve historical evidence but cannot manufacture current operational authority.

## Contract Gates
