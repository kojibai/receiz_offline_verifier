# Receiz v53.0.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 14, 2026
Status: Locked for `v53.0.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v53.0.0`. It carries forward the existing file-authoritative, route-deterministic, settlement-truth, and governed-market model from `v52.5.0` while recording public market asset data routes, indexable market surfaces, persisted conformance history, micro-dollar prediction resolution, touch-safe market interactions, stronger public proof/player metadata, signed-in account passkey attachment, first-class Enterprise entitlement handling, simpler beginner-first product framing, and public-key-based release attestation as explicit non-regression behavior at release time.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, and visible version-surface alignment only. The verifier trust contract remains unchanged relative to `v52.0.0`.

## Core Authority Boundaries

The release does not relax the core Receiz authority model:

- The file remains the authority for artifact truth.
- Deterministic manifests remain the authority for historical route truth.
- Ledger-backed settlement remains the authority for payment truth.
- Market remains the governed execution layer around those truths rather than a replacement for them.
- Public market routes, public JSON feeds, proof metadata, player hydration, and search/share surfaces must continue to reflect those authorities rather than invent alternative truth systems.
- Offline verifier entrypoints, release metadata, and public release documents remain transport and governance around those boundaries, not replacements for them.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. Market Must Stay Governed While Becoming Public

- The deterministic quote engine, quote-embedded spread behavior, replayable quote evidence, partial share-certificate custody, and dedicated market conformance model from `v52.0.0` and `v52.5.0` must remain intact.
- `/market`, `/market/assets/[receizId]`, `/api/market/public-snapshot`, and `/api/market/assets/[receizId]` must continue to behave as public, indexable, machine-readable surfaces for governed market state.
- Market share routes must continue to carry asset-specific metadata, Open Graph treatment, and dataset context instead of falling back to generic page treatment.

### 2. Market Touch, Viewer, And Playback Flows Must Stay Coherent

- Touch-aware pill actions, richer chart context, overlay tooling, tighter metric/action layouts, and direct viewer entry from market surfaces must remain available where live artifact context exists.
- Public market assets must continue to hydrate cleanly into live-player playback flows rather than dead-ending on share routes.
- Global player refreshes must preserve dock theme and viewer hydration hints when market playback updates.

### 3. Market Conformance Must Stay Historical, Not Disposable

- Market conformance snapshots and daily rollups must continue to persist through the snapshot tables, history helpers, and history/rollup APIs.
- The live conformance route must continue to record snapshot IDs, recorded timestamps, and attestation state instead of returning only a disposable one-off response.
- Governance evidence for market conformance must remain replayable beyond a single request.

### 4. Prediction Resolution Must Stay Precise And Formalized

- Prediction comparisons must continue to use micro-dollar precision rather than rounded cents for borderline outcomes.
- Wagers must continue to reject closed or expired contracts reliably.
- Expiry and finalization must continue to resolve from pulse or timestamp with explicit forced-finalization and ambiguity-handling paths rather than one brittle code path.
- Borderline price comparisons and ambiguous finalize states must remain deterministically handled.

### 5. Identity And Entitlement Boundaries Must Respect Ownership

- Public passkey registration must not silently claim or enroll an already-existing email account.
- Signed-in account or profile-owner surfaces must remain the path for attaching Face ID / passkeys to an existing email account.
- Staged email claims must remain pending until the passkey ceremony succeeds.
- Enterprise must remain a first-class entitlement across shared plan, session, world, and receipt logic rather than falling through lower-plan behavior.

### 6. Public Product Framing Must Stay Beginner-First

- Public onboarding must continue to center Verify, Seal, Record, and Public Twin as the first concepts.
- World must remain framed as an advanced expansion layer rather than the default introductory concept.
- `/receiz-world` must continue to redirect to `/public-twin#world`.
- `/login` must remain a `noindex` developer embed surface while `/signin` remains the normal user path.
- Business and Automation upgrade framing must remain coherent, with Business presented as the more common next step.

### 7. Proof And Market Metadata Must Stay Machine-Readable

- Proof pages, live-player pages, market asset pages, and sitemap surfaces must continue to emit stronger machine-readable metadata rather than stopping at human-facing text only.
- Structured data, derived proof metadata helpers, and Open Graph treatment must remain specific to the public proof/player/market surface being shared.
- Machine consumers must remain able to discover public proof and market truth through metadata, not just manual page reading.

### 8. Release Governance And Version Truth

- Release-scoped note and product-truth documents are required for `v53.0.0`.
- Release-governed version surfaces must move together across current-release docs, verifier-contract docs, and shipped offline HTML entrypoints.
- New release attestations must be signed with `Ed25519` using a signing key ID that matches an active key in `public/governance/governance-keyring.v1.json`; legacy HMAC verification is retained only for older artifacts.
- This repository records the public release documents and shipped HTML version markers for that lockstep requirement.

## Allowed Evolution

Implementation details may change after `v53.0.0` only if they preserve the authority boundaries and locked release behaviors above. If a future change materially alters one of these truths, it should ship under a new explicit release note and updated product-truth freeze.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v53.0.0` if it:

- regresses market back into a UI-only feature instead of a governed execution layer with canonical quote behavior
- removes or degrades the public market routes, public JSON feeds, or asset-specific metadata that make market shareable and machine-readable
- breaks touch-safe market actions, viewer entry, playback hydration, or market value-pill continuity
- downgrades market conformance history back into disposable live-only responses
- reintroduces rounded-cent ambiguity, brittle expiry/finalization rules, or nondeterministic prediction-resolution paths
- weakens account ownership by allowing public passkey enrollment to silently claim existing email users
- causes Enterprise entitlements to fall through lower-plan logic in shared runtime behavior
- makes the public front door more jargon-heavy or turns `/login` back into the primary normal-user sign-in path
- changes release truth without updating the versioned release docs and aligned version surfaces
