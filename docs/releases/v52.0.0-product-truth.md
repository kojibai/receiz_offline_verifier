# Receiz v52.0.0 Product Truth

Release date: April 13, 2026
Status: Locked for `v52.0.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v52.0.0`. It carries forward the existing file-authoritative, route-deterministic, and settlement-truth platform model while recording deterministic market execution, replayable quote evidence, bounded pressure inputs, partial share-certificate custody, first-class market conformance, and lighter premium market runtime paths as explicit non-regression behavior at release time.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, and visible version-surface alignment only. The verifier trust contract remains unchanged relative to `v51.0.0`.

## Core Authority Boundaries

The release does not relax the core Receiz authority model:

- The file remains the authority for artifact truth.
- Deterministic manifests remain the authority for historical route truth.
- Ledger-backed settlement remains the authority for payment truth.
- Market quotes, spread capture, custody movement, and conformance reporting must execute around those authorities rather than replace them.
- Quote pressure inputs may shape execution inside bounded rules, but they must not turn the market into opaque or non-replayable pricing behavior.
- Offline verifier entrypoints, release metadata, and public release documents remain transport and governance around those boundaries, not replacements for them.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. Market Execution Must Stay Deterministic

- A canonical quote model version must continue to define market execution behavior.
- Market execution must continue to resolve from deterministic inputs into reference mid, quote mid, bid, ask, spread basis points, and shift basis points.
- Execution-preview surfaces must continue to align with those bid/ask quotes when quote data is available.

### 2. Spread Must Remain Quote-Embedded And Replayable

- Market spread capture must remain part of the quote itself instead of a separately layered visible runtime fee.
- Buys and sells must continue to persist replayable quote evidence through trade metadata and quote snapshot fields.
- A completed trade must remain explainable later from the persisted quote surface that produced it.

### 3. Market Pressure Must Stay Bounded And Explainable

- Inventory imbalance, recent flow, and volatility pressure must remain explicit bounded inputs into quote shaping.
- Spread and shift behavior must continue to obey hard-cap guards rather than drift into ad hoc economics.
- The system must remain able to explain how pressure changed a live quote without abandoning deterministic behavior.

### 4. Share Certificates Must Support Partial Custody Motion

- Share certificates must continue to support partial withdrawals rather than all-or-nothing movement.
- Certificates must continue to track remaining balances on the certificate itself.
- Redeposit flows must continue to support all-or-part returns into platform custody.
- Share quantity and market value continuity must remain coherent across export and import paths.

### 5. Market Conformance Must Remain First-Class

- Market must continue to have its own conformance suite, badge, history, and rollup endpoints.
- Market conformance must continue to validate quote determinism, bounded spread and shift behavior, execution-preview alignment, persisted quote snapshot fields, and trace inclusion for required source files.
- Release attestation and broader trust surfaces must continue to treat market as its own governed suite rather than hiding it behind settlement.

### 6. Premium Market Surfaces Must Stay Lighter Under Load

- Pulse-only consumers must continue to use pulse-only update paths when they do not render countdowns.
- Asset-scoped market pills must continue to resolve lightweight lanes before waking the full market snapshot.
- Market bootstrap work must remain aggressively parallelized.
- Market time formatting must remain deterministic so `/market` does not regress into locale/timezone hydration mismatch.

### 7. Release Governance And Version Truth

- Release-scoped note and product-truth documents are required for `v52.0.0`.
- Release-governed version surfaces must move together across current-release docs and shipped offline HTML entrypoints.
- This repository records the public release documents and shipped HTML version markers for that lockstep requirement.

## Allowed Evolution

Implementation details may change after `v52.0.0` only if they preserve the authority boundaries and locked release behaviors above. If a future change materially alters one of these truths, it should ship under a new explicit release note and updated product-truth freeze.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v52.0.0` if it:

- removes the canonical quote model as the authority for market execution behavior
- reintroduces a separate visible runtime fee story on top of execution instead of quote-embedded spread capture
- makes quote pressure inputs opaque, unbounded, or no longer explainable from persisted evidence
- breaks partial share-certificate withdrawal, remaining-balance tracking, or partial redeposit continuity
- downgrades market conformance, release attestation, or trust-row treatment back into a settlement-only story
- brings pulse-only consumers, market pills, or market bootstrap back to wasteful rerender or hydration behavior
- changes release truth without updating the versioned release docs and aligned version surfaces
