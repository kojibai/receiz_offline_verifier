# Receiz v76.0.0 Product Truth

Release date: May 6, 2026
Status: Locked for `v76.0.0` release prep

## Purpose

This document defines the product-truth freeze for Receiz `v76.0.0`, the World Agent / Market Twin / Public Proof / Status release after the `v75.0.0` candidate.

`v76.0.0` preserves the `v70.0.0` public alpha law, `v74.0.0` Receiz Key / identity-artwork baseline, and `v75.0.0` local-proof/file-restore law while adding world-agent revenue loops, mandate-safe Market Twin money actions, first-class Public Proof registry behavior, and public/developer trust-surface framing.

For this offline verifier repository, the release is represented as current-release documentation, canonical release artifacts, shipped HTML version markers, and package-metadata alignment only. The verifier proof-format and producer-payload contract remain unchanged.

## Core Product Truth

`v76.0.0` carries forward `v70.0.0` public alpha law, `v74.0.0` identity baseline, and `v75.0.0` local-continuity law:

> Identity is local. Proof is in the file. Verified truth is append-only. `/<username>` is canonical profile truth. Settlement and Reserve preserve funded causality. The network propagates and appends; it does not get to downgrade stronger proof already held by the device.

## Locked Release Surface

### 1. World Concierge Remains Direct, Agents Are Bounded and Additive

- Concierge replies continue over the existing direct Responses streaming path.
- Agents SDK is used for agentic work, tool execution, handoffs, tracing, and orchestration where it adds capability.
- The transition preserves user-visible response continuity.
- Raw internal trace internals are not exposed on public surfaces.

### 2. World Toolbelt Produces Revenue Artifacts

- Lead research, contact enrichment, CRM sync, outreach drafting, outreach sending, follow-up campaigns, reply classification, payment path generation, booking path generation, proof ledger entries, handoffs, and Sales Brain routing are part of the official toolbelt scope.
- Tool actions must be observable as business artifacts and missions, not opaque side effects.
- Revenue attribution remains tied to these surfaced mission outcomes.

### 3. Public-Safe Revenue Trace UX

- Public-facing trace exposes task-level summaries only.
- Allowed summaries include researched leads, imported contacts, drafted messages, sent messages, held actions, and next recommended revenue action.
- Raw model traces are never required for public proof of work.

### 4. Campaign Continuity Is Deterministic and Healable

- Campaign follow-up defaults to email unless user-configured connected-app webhook destination is valid.
- Legacy invalid webhook campaigns are repairable back to email delivery.
- Autopilot/brief loops keep mission continuity even when connection failures occur.

### 5. Market Twin Is Mandate-Safe, Persistent, and Replay-Honest

- Market Twin opens from cached market truth and preserves saved authority without shell remount.
- Movement explanations, mandate checks, and rate/state protections are explicit in the surface.
- Authority settings persist: owner mandate, approval/auto mode, order caps, daily caps, profit sweep preference.
- Append-only intent rows are preserved.
- Repair migration covers environments with missing columns from prior create-table behavior.
- Replay logic is based on deterministic market history and truthful labelling (simulated vs replay).
- Win-rate math is computed from actual replayed outcomes.

### 6. Public Proof Becomes a First-Class Registry Surface

- Public Proof supports URL observe, proof record creation, ID/URL resolution, creator lookup, claim/verify/seal/dispute flows, identity verification, OAuth channel control, rights assertions, and public registry feed discovery.
- Public Proof UI supports compact command-first mobile surfaces and deterministic state transitions.
- Public proof routes remain aligned to proof/state contract and do not expose private controls.

### 7. Wallet Settlement and Value-State Framing Are Corrected

- Non-subscription reserve and payment-credit handling is fixed.
- Running reserve balances are preserved and surfaced safely.
- Public and private ledger behavior stays partitioned and auditable.
- Value-state framing remains settlement-ledger-first while preserving funded Reserve causality.

### 8. Status and Profile/Auth Surfaces Stay Safe and Useful

- Public and developer Status include high-value safe routes: OIDC/OAuth discovery, all conformance suites, core Receiz proof routes, public profile, Public Proof, Market read surfaces, World public/profile/stream, Calendar embed, signer keyring, public wallet/action ledgers, payment embeds, and Status itself.
- Status excludes admin-only, webhook-only, internal worker-only, private-session-only, and privileged-issuer routes.
- Profile/auth continuity preserves restored/local binds across PBI, Receiz Key, passkey, and bridge redemption when already-verified truth exists.

### 9. Release-Surface Lockstep

- Package metadata, shipped HTML version markers, release docs, and current-release readme/release-note pointers must move together to `v76.0.0`.
- `v75.0.0` assets and historical proofs remain for archive truth.

### 10. Mobile and UX Continuity Rules

- World and Market mobile polish preserves no-zoom input behavior.
- Four-across compact pills and popover command surfaces remain the public interaction pattern.
- Bottom-chrome spacing and first-paint continuity stay deterministic.
- Public interfaces remain useful for business outcome progression without exposing internal operator controls.

## Important Non-Changes

- The verifier proof-format and producer-payload contract remain unchanged.
- Trusted-signature success remains `signatureV4`-required for verified outcomes.
- Effective anchor context remains required for verified outcomes.
- Groth16 fields remain required and only real `g16:` proof payloads are accepted.
- The `v64.0.0` value-loop invariants remain in force.
- The `v70.0.0` public alpha law, `v74.0.0` Receiz Key baseline, and `v75.0.0` local-continuity law remain in force.

## Non-Regression Rule

A change is out of bounds for `v76.0.0` if it:

- routes public-safe surfaces through raw internal traces
- weakens direct concierge continuity by breaking the direct response path
- introduces mandate bypass for Market Twin money actions
- drops persisted Market Twin authority or intent rows
- changes campaign follow-up defaults away from email without a valid webhook
- exposes private or internal routes on Status
- replaces stronger local proof with stale transport echoes
- changes value-state framing so reserve/payment credit no longer tracks running reserve truth
- reintroduces undefined release version drift between package metadata, docs, and shipped entrypoints
