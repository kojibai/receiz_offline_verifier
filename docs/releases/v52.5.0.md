# Receiz v52.5.0

Release date: April 13, 2026

## Overview

`v52.5.0` is the follow-on release to `v52.0.0`: it takes the first market-governed release and hardens it into a more coherent execution system with a premium operator-grade shell.

`v52.0.0` established market as a governed primitive. `v52.5.0` keeps that foundation intact and carries the next hardening pass now present in the repository: deterministic quote math, persisted quote evidence, bounded pressure/spread behavior, partial share-certificate custody, a first-class market conformance standard, lighter runtime paths, and a tighter premium market experience that still respects the platform's existing file-authoritative and settlement-authoritative primitives.

The product-truth freeze for this release is defined in `docs/releases/v52.5.0-product-truth.md`.

## What Defines v52.5.0

- Market execution resolves through a canonical deterministic quote engine rather than a visible additive fee layer.
- Asset trades persist replayable quote evidence including bid, ask, quote mid, spread, shift, and pressure inputs.
- Platform sustainability is governed by bounded spread/pressure math plus execution guards instead of ad hoc trade tolls.
- Share certificates now support partial withdrawal, remaining balances, and partial redeposit.
- Market has its own conformance suite, badge, history surfaces, and release-governed documentation.
- Market pills on profile, artifact, and live-player surfaces are lighter, smaller, sigil-driven, and no longer depend on a full market snapshot just to display one asset.
- The pill chart surface is now a movable floating desk card with no backdrop blur, rather than a locked modal-like popover.
- The launch panel now creates sealed records/assets and ventures inline instead of kicking users out to other product surfaces.
- Market asset deep links now resolve to a dedicated market share route with asset-specific Open Graph and Twitter cards.
- Release-governed version surfaces now move together to `v52.5.0`.

## Release Framing

Receiz has been building toward one consistent model across proof, settlement, world, and market:

- the file remains authoritative
- manifests remain authoritative for route truth
- settlement remains authoritative for ledger/payment truth
- market becomes the governed execution layer around those truths

`v52.0.0` made that model explicit. `v52.5.0` makes it hold up better in product reality.

## Highlights

### 1. Deterministic Quote Engine

Market execution is now explicitly modeled rather than implied.

This release locks asset execution to a quote engine that computes:

- reference mid
- quote mid
- bid
- ask
- spread basis points
- shift basis points
- bounded pressure inputs

That change matters for both product and business reasons. Users no longer experience market economics as a bolted-on fee. Operators no longer need to explain execution from vibes. The trade can be replayed from the quote.

### 2. Spread-Governed Sustainability

The business model is now closer to how a real market should feel.

Instead of surfacing a visible runtime fee on top of execution:

- the live quote carries the spread behavior
- buy/sell previews resolve against the same quote surface used by execution
- bounded pressure and hard-cap guards help keep the platform protected
- persisted trade evidence explains why the trade settled where it did

This is cleaner for users, stronger for governance, and more coherent with the rest of Receiz.

### 3. Replayable Trade Evidence

Every serious market needs more than a final fill price.

`v52.5.0` persists the exact quote context needed to audit an executed trade later, including:

- `quote_model_version`
- `reference_mid_usd_cents`
- `quote_mid_usd_cents`
- `bid_price_usd_cents`
- `ask_price_usd_cents`
- `spread_bps`
- `shift_bps`
- `pressure_inputs`

That means market execution is not just deterministic at runtime. It is explainable after the fact.

### 4. Share Certificates Became Real Custody Movement

Share certificates were already a compelling idea. This release makes them materially more useful.

Users can now:

- withdraw a partial quantity instead of all-or-nothing
- see the remaining certificate balance
- redeposit all or part of that balance
- preserve share quantity and value coherence across custody movement

This turns certificates from a novelty into a real custody primitive inside the platform.

### 5. Premium Surface, Lower Runtime Waste

The market shell is darker, sharper, and more premium, but the more important change is that it wastes less work.

This release hardens the runtime in several places:

- pulse-only market surfaces no longer rerender on a 50ms countdown cadence
- market pills use an asset-scoped path first instead of waking the full desk snapshot immediately
- market bootstrap is more parallel
- `/market` formatting is hydration-safe
- the market route applies immersive chrome more reliably
- the market audio dock now inherits the active asset palette instead of falling back to the global blue skin

The result is a desk that feels alive without feeling sloppy.

### 6. Market Value Pill Is Now A Real Floating Tool

The value pill work became much more deliberate in this release.

It now:

- uses the asset's actual sigil instead of generic iconography
- stays smaller and more refined across profile, artifact, and player surfaces
- opens into a tighter single-view floating card
- supports direct buy/sell inside the surface
- can escalate seamlessly into the full market desk
- no longer blurs the page behind it
- can be dragged anywhere on the screen like a proper floating market tool

That change is small visually, but it materially improves the product feel.

### 7. Launch Stays Inside The Market

The launch panel is no longer a thin handoff.

From inside the market panel, users can now:

- record and seal a text memory
- attach and seal a file-backed asset
- create a venture lane directly from the desk

This keeps launch behavior coherent with the market instead of forcing unnecessary navigation jumps.

### 8. Market Media Experience Is Contextual

The asset action on the market card is now media-aware.

- audio and video hand off to the same bottom player pattern used elsewhere in product
- market audio playback now hands the global player a real market queue so tracks can crossfade into the next song without stopping at the current asset
- the market-only audio dock now skins itself from the active asset instead of looking like the generic player everywhere else
- images open into framed viewer treatment
- PDFs and files open into the document reader
- EPUBs open into the ebook reader
- market header actions can copy or share an official market-asset link directly from the desk

That keeps the market aligned with the existing Receiz media primitives instead of inventing a separate behavior stack.

### 9. Market Conformance Is First-Class

Settlement conformance was no longer enough to represent what market had become.

Market now ships with its own governed surfaces:

- `/market/conformance`
- `/api/market/conformance`
- `/api/market/conformance/badge`
- `/api/market/conformance/history`
- `/api/market/conformance/history/rollups`

The release also carries the Vercel/Next tracing work needed so the market conformance suite can access the required source files in production rather than failing on missing trace inputs.

### 10. Market Share Surfaces Became Official

Shared market assets now have a real public route rather than a generic desk URL.

- `/market/assets/[receizId]` opens the market desk focused on one live asset
- the route carries asset-specific metadata instead of inheriting the noindex generic market page
- Open Graph and Twitter images now present a dedicated market card that reads as a live, official financial surface rather than a recycled artifact card

That makes market sharing feel intentional, trustworthy, and legible off-platform.

## Public Summary

Receiz `v52.5.0` is the refinement release after `v52.0.0`. Assets continue trading through a deterministic quote engine with replayable quote evidence, certificates support partial custody movement, the market desk and value-pill surfaces are more premium and lighter at runtime, market playback now carries a real asset-colored queue flow, and market now ships with stronger conformance and share-ready trust surfaces.
